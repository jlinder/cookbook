How to Make Your Own Chili Powder
    or "Some Like it Hot" Chili Powder

Assemble the following ingredients:

For mildness and flavor:

    3 tbsp  Ancho chili powder
    3 tbsp  New Mexico chili powder

For heat:

    3 to 5 tsp  Chiles de Arbol, Cayenne or other hot chili powder

For flavor:

    2 tbsp  ground cumin
    2 tbsp  garlic powder
    1 tbsp  ground oregano (Mexican oregano, if you can get it)

Mix the powders.  Store in an air-tight container until use.
