Avocado Vichyssoise

Ingredients:

  2 tbs Butter
  1-2 Avocado
  3 Large Potatoes
  3 Leeks
  4 cups Stock
  Cilantro

Directions:

Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a large pot. Add 3 peeled and cubed potatoes and 3 trimmed and chopped leeks. Cook for about 3 minutes, stirring, until softened. Add 4 cups stock. Boil, cover, lower the heat and simmer until vegetables are tender, about 20 minutes. Pur√©e, then let cool. Stir in coarsely chopped flesh of 1 or 2 avocados before serving. Garnish: Chopped cilantro.
