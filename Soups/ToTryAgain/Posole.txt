Posole

Ingredients

  1 cup Kidney beans boiled
  1/2 cup Corn kernels boiled
  2-3 tbsp Oil
  1 medium Onion, chopped
  1 tbsp Garlic, chopped
  1 medium Tomato, chopped
  1/4 cup Tomato puree
  1/4 tsp Cumin powder
  1 tsp Red chilli powder
  1/2 tsp Black pepper powder
  Salt to taste
  1/2 tsp Oregano
  3/4 cup Vegetable stock
  Fresh coriander leaves, chopped
  1 Lemon

Directions

1. Heat oil in a non-stick pan. Add onion.

2. Add garlic and sauté for a minute. Add tomato and sauté for 2-3 minutes. Add tomato puree and mix well.

3. Add kidney beans and mix again. Add cumin powder, red chilli powder, pepper powder, salt and oregano.

4. Mix well and add the vegetable stock. Stir to mix and simmer for 5-7 minutes.

5. Add corn kernels to the pan and mix. Cook for 2-3 minutes.

6. Crush the kidney beans and corn with the back of a ladle and stir to mix. Transfer soup into a serving bowl, cilantro and serve hot with lemon slices.
