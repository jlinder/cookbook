Spiced Oatmeal

Ingredients

  2 cups water
  2 tsp fenugreek
  1/2 tsp cinnamon
  1/2 tsp ginger powder
  1/4 tsp star anise
  1/4 tsp mace
  1/4 tsp salt
  1 tbsp butter
  1 cup 1-minute oatmeal (Quaker Oats)
  
Directions

Place the water in a pot with the spices, salt and butter. Heat the water over high heat to boil.

Once the water is boiling, add the oatmeal and cook. Stir frequently. Cook for about 5 minutes or until the oatmeal begins to stick to the bottom of the pan when stirring.
