Ceviche

Ingredients

  2 lbs raw shrimp
  2 regular tomatoes
  1 cup chopped cilantro
  2 green onions (white and green parts), chopped
  6 lemons, juiced
  1 medium white onion
  1/2 orange
  2 tbsp salt
  1/4 cup olive oil
  3 cloves garlic
  1 tbsp all-purpose seasoning

Directions:

Juice lemons
Dice onions
Place onions, lemon, 1 tbsp salt and olive oil in a bowl.  Make sure onions are fully immersed in lemon juice.
De-vein and de-shell shrimp.
Place shrimp in pot and add water until they are fully immersed.  Mix in 1 tbsp salt, 1/2 cup cilantro, crushed garlic and all-purpose seasoning.  Bring to a boil and cook for about 3 minutes until shrimp turn a reddish color.  Do not overcook the shrimp!  Strain about 1 cup of the water and discard the rest.
Dice one tomato and grate one tomato into bowl.
Add shrimp and the cup of strained water into bowl.
Squeeze half orange into bowl.
Add remaining cilantro.
Cool in refrigerator for 2 hours and serve
