Ceviche

Ingredients

  2 green onions (white and green parts), chopped
  1 medium white onion, diced
  6 lemons, juiced
  2 tbsp salt, split
  1/4 cup olive oil
  2 lbs raw shrimp, de-veined and de-shelled
  1 cup chopped cilantro, split
  3 cloves garlic, crushed
  1 tbsp all-purpose seasoning
  2 regular tomatoes
  1/2 orange, juiced

Directions:

1. Place onions, lemon juice, 1 tbsp salt and olive oil in a large bowl.  Make sure onions are fully immersed in lemon juice.

2. Place shrimp in pot and add water until they are fully immersed.  Mix in 1 tbsp salt, 1/2 cup cilantro, crushed garlic and all-purpose seasoning.  Bring to a boil and cook for about 3 minutes until shrimp turn a reddish color.  Do not overcook the shrimp!  Separate the shrimp from the water retaining 1 cup of the water and discarding the rest.

3. Grate one tomato, dice the other and place into the bowl. Add shrimp, the cup of strained water, the orange juice, and the remaining cilantro.

4. Cool in refrigerator for 2 hours and serve.
