Orange Broccoli

1 tablespoon olive oil
Zest of 1/2 orange, peeled in long strips
1/4 tablespoon crushed red pepper flakes
1 bunch broccoli, cut into small pieces, stalks peeled and thinly sliced (6 cups)
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt

In a large, heavy skillet, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the orange zest and red pepper and let the mixture sizzle for 30 seconds. Add the broccoli and cook, stirring occasionally, for 5 minutes or until tender. Sprinkle with the salt.



Yield: 4 servings

NUTRITION PER SERVING
CALORIES 62(54% from fat); FAT 4g (sat 1g); CHOLESTEROL 0mg; CALCIUM 54mg; CARBOHYDRATE 6g; SODIUM 317mg; PROTEIN 3mg; FIBER 3g; IRON 1mg
