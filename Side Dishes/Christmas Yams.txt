Christmas Yams

Ingredients

  3-4 lbs yams
  2 tbsp butter
  2 tbsp brown sugar
  1 tbsp cinnamon
  1/2 tbsp allspice 
  1/2 tbsp ginger
  1 tsp clove
  1 tsp nutmeg
  salt

Directions

Place potatoes in a pot and cover with water. Bring the water to a boil and salt it. Cook potatoes until tender 15 minutes. Drain the potatoes and return to hot pot and mash with butter, sugar, cinnamon, allspice, ginger, clove and nutmeg. Season with salt to taste.
