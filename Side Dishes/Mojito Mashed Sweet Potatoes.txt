Mojito Mashed Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients

  2 sweet potatoes, peeled and sliced
  Salt
  2 tablespoons butter
  2 tablespoons sugar
  2 shots rum or spiced rum
  2 limes, juiced
  Handful mint leaves, chopped

Directions

Place potatoes in a small pot and cover with water. Bring the water to a boil and salt it. Cook potatoes until tender 15 minutes. Drain the potatoes and return to hot pot and mash with butter, sugar, rum, lime and mint. Season the Mojito Mash with salt, to taste.
