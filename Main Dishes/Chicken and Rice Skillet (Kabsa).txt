Chicken & Rice Skillet (Kabsa)

Ingredients:

    6 tbsp. butter
    1 (2.5 to 3 lb.) chicken, cut up
    1 large onion, chopped
    5 garlic cloves, minced
    1/4 cup tomato sauce or puree
    2 medium tomatoes, chopped
    2 medium carrots, grated
    3 cups chicken broth
    grated peel of 1 orange
    8 whole cloves
    5 cardamom pods or 1 tsp. cardamom seeds
    2 cinnamon sticks (4 inches total)
    Salt and ground black pepper to taste
    1 cup rice (long grain, basmatti, ?)
    1/4 cup raisins
    1/4 cup toasted sliced or slivered almonds

Directions:

Melt 4 tbsp. butter in a large skillet.  Brown chicken pieces, then remove them from the pan.

Melt remaining 2 tbsp. butter in the skillet.  Add the onion and saute until tender.  Stir in garlic and tomato sauce/puree.  Simmer of low heat 1 minute to blend flavors.  Add tomatoes and carrots.  Cook 1 minute.

Add broth.  Return chicken to skillet and bring to a boil.  Reduce heat and cover.  Simmer over low heat 15 minutes.

Add orange peel cloves, cardamom, cinamon stick, salt and pepper.  Continue simmering over low heat 15 minutes.

Add rice to pot and stir in.  Simmer until rice is done (take the time for cooking the rice and add 10 minutes).

Remove from heat.  Stir in raisings and almonds.  Serve.

NOTES:

- Better the second day
- Don't cook the spices too long
