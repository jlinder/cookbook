Chandi Korma (chicken korma)

Ingredients

  Chicken cut into 12 pieces 1 kilogram
  Silver warq for garnish
  Oil 3 tablespoons
  Onion sliced 3 medium
  Ginger paste 1 tablespoon
  Garlic paste 1 tablespoon
  Green chilli paste 1 tablespoon
  Almonds ground 1/4 cup
  Yogurt 1 cup
  Almond milk 1 cup
  White pepper powder 1 teaspoon
  Green cardamom powder 1/2 teaspoon
  Rose petals 1/2 teaspoon
  Salt 1 teaspoon
  Almonds slivered 5-6
  Pistachios slivered 5-6

Directions

1. Heat oil in a pan, add onions and sauté till golden brown. Add ginger and garlic pastes and stir. Add green chilli paste and sauté for five minutes. 

2. Add almond paste, yogurt and almond milk. Cook on low heat for ten to twenty minutes. 

3. Add white pepper powder and stir. Add chicken pieces and cook further on low heat for ten minutes, stirring occasionally.

4. Pour in one cup of water and simmer for another five minutes.

5. Add green cardamom powder, rose petal powder and salt, simmer for two minutes and remove from heat. Garnish with silver varq, almond and pistachio slivers. Serve hot.
