Gluten-Free Meatballs

Ingredients:

  1.5 lbs lean beef or pork
  3/4 cup parmesan cheese
  1 large slightly beaten egg
  1/2 cup gluten-free breadcrumbs / quinoa / crumbled tortilla
    chips / ground flax
  2 tbsp minced garlic
  2 tsp salt
  1 tsp black pepper
  1/2 cup milk
  1 tsp dried oregano
  2 tsp dried parsley

Directions:

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
2. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
3. Shape mixture into small- or medium-sized meatballs (about 2")
4. Place meatballs on a baking sheet
5. Bake for 25 minutes.
