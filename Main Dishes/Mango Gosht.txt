Mango Gosht

Tangy marinated mutton cooked with raw mangoes, an Andhra speciality.

Ingredients

  1.3 lbs boneless lamb, 1 inch pieces
  3/4 cup yogurt
  1 tsp dry mango powder (amchur)
  1 tsp turmeric powder
  4 tbsp oil
  1 tsp mustard seeds
  8-10 curry leaves
  4-5 Cloves
  1" stick cinnamon
  3 large onions, grated
  1 tbsp ginger paste
  1 tbsp coriander powder
  1 tbsp red chilli powder
  1/2 raw mango, peeled and chopped 
  Salt to taste

Directions

1. Marinate the mutton pieces in a mixture of yogurt, dry mango powder and turmeric powder for one hour preferably in the refrigerator.

2. Heat oil in a thick-bottomed pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, cloves and cinnamon. Once mustard seeds crackle add onions and saut√© till onions are translucent.

3. Add marinated meat pieces and ginger paste. Cook on high heat, stirring constantly. Add coriander powder, red chilli powder and raw mango pieces.

4. Stir and cook for half a minute. Add two cups of water and bring to a boil. Cover the pan and let it simmer for half an hour or till meat pieces are tender.

5. Season with salt and again cook on a low heat for ten minutes or till the gravy thickens. Serve hot with bread of your choice.
