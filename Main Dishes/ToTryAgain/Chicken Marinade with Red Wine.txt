Chicken Marinade with Red Wine

Ingredients

  2 cups dry red wine
  1/2 cup olive oil
  1/4 cup soy or teriyaki sauce
  4 scallions, sliced
  2 cloves garlic, minced
  2 tablespoons brown sugar
  1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  1 teaspoon fresh ginger, grated

Directions

Combine all ingredients in a medium saucepan. Let boil for 10 minutes. Allow to cool. Maybe stored in the refrigerator for several days.

Yield: Makes about 2 1/2 cups
