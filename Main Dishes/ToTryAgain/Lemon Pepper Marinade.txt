Lemon Pepper Marinade

Ingredients

  1/4 cup lemon juice
  1/4 cup light cooking oil
  1/2 teaspoon black peppercorns, cracked
  1/4 cup water
  1/2 teaspoon fresh dill
  1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

Combine all ingredients and mix well. Makes about 1/2 cup.
