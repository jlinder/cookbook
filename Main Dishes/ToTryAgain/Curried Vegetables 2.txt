Ingredients:

- 2 table spoons olive oil
- 5 black cardamom pods
- 5 cloves garlic
- 2 onionsÂ 
- 1 can coconut milk
- 2 tbsp curry powder (https://github.com/jlinder/cookbook/blob/master/Spice%20Blends/Alton%20Browns%20Basic%20Curry%20Powder.txt)
- 1 tsp ginger root powder
- 3 sweet potatoes
- 2 very large carrots
- 3 zucchini
- 3 yellow squash
- 1 can chickpeas
- 1 cup water
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp garam masala (https://github.com/jlinder/cookbook/blob/master/Spice%20Blends/Garam%20Masala%20Spice%20Mix.txt)

Steps:

1. Crack open the black cardamom pods (like with the butt of a knife). Place olive oil and cardamom pods in a pot. Heat on medium heat until the pods start popping.

2. Add the onions and simmer on low-medium heat for until translucent (perhaps 8 minutes).

3. Add the garlic and simmer for 2 minutes.

4. Add the coconut milk, curry powder and ginger powder. Mix and simmer for 5 minutes.

5. Add the vegetables and water. Bring back to a boil and simmer for 10-15 minutes.

6. Add the salt and garam masala. Simmer for 2 minutes.
