Curried Veggies

Ingredients

  2 tbsp oil
  2 onions sliced
  5 cloves garlic minced
  2 inch piece ginger grated
  1 tbsp curry powder
  7 whole cloves
  1 3-inch cinnamon stick
  2 bay leaves
  1 (~14 oz) can coconut milk
  8 cups vegetables 1-inch cubed/cut (green beans, zucchini, potatoes, etc.)
  16 oz beans (chickpeas, lentils, red beans, etc.)
  2 cups vegetable broth or water
  salt and pepper to taste

Directions

1. Sauté the onions for 3-5 minutes until soft.

2. Add the garlic and ginger. Sauté for 5 minutes.

3. Add the remainder of the ingredients. Bring to a boil, then lower to a simmer for 20 minutes.
