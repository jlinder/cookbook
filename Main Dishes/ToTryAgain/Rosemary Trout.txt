Rosemary Trout

Ingredients:

  2 whole trout
  1 onion
  1/2 fresh squeezed lemon
  2 1/2 tbsp olive oil
  2 tsp chopped fresh rosemary
  4 large sprigs fresh rosemary
  salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

1. Mix onion, chopped rosemary, lemon juice and 1 tsp olive oil in bowl. Season with salt and pepper.

2. Open trout like a book.  Sprinkle with coarse salt and pepper.  Place 2 rosemary springs in each trout.  Fold closed.

3. Heat a small skillet and a large skillet on medium-high.  Once hot, to the small skillet add the onion mixture.  To the large skillet add 1.5 tablespoons oil, wait for oil to heat, then add the 2 trout.

4. Cook the onions until tender and translucent.  Cook trout until brown outside and just opaque in center, about 4 minutes per side.
