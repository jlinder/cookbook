Mayonnaise

Ingredients

  1 egg yolk
  1 tbsp lemon juice
  1 tsp cold water
  1 cup vegetable oil
  1/2 tsp salt

  Optional:
    1/2 tsp pepper
    dill weed to taste
    other spices to taste

Directions

Place egg yolk in small mixing bowl and whisk. 

Add lemon juice and water. Whisk together.

Slowly add vegetable oil to the mixture while whisking continuously.

Whisk salt and other optional ingredients into the mixture.
